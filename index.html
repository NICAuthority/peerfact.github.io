PeerFact

Check It before you Rep It!

Project Details

The goal of PeerFact is to reduce the amount of misinformation that is present on social networks. People's opinions can be formed based on the posts they see on their social networks. I believe it is our duty to make sure that information be as accurate as possible to allow for a more informed public. This project is not meant to be partisan, it is meant to be factual.

How it Works

The PeerFact plugin will scan every post that appears in your news feed and automatically flag posts that are being shared from known fake news sites. It will also point out articles that are satirical in nature.
